About Us

Meet Dr. Majid and learn about our journey

Dr. Majid

Founder & Lead Physiotherapist

I am a physiotherapist with 25 years of experience. I established the first physiotherapy clinic in North Kashmir in the year 2000. I have worked in reputed hospitals and organizations like SKIMS, HOSMAT Bangalore, Handicap International's project funded by the European Commission, Government Medical College Baramulla, and served as an online consultant for HealthifyMe.

My passion for physiotherapy began with a desire to help people recover from injuries and manage chronic conditions without relying solely on medication. Over the years, I've had the privilege of helping thousands of patients regain their mobility, reduce pain, and improve their quality of life.

My approach combines traditional physiotherapy techniques with modern evidence-based practices, ensuring that each patient receives the most effective treatment for their specific condition.

Our Mission

Committed to improving quality of life through evidence-based care

At Dr. Majid's Physiotherapy and Weight Loss Clinic, our mission is to provide personalized, evidence-based care that addresses the root causes of pain, mobility limitations, and weight management challenges.

We are committed to:

  • Delivering high-quality physiotherapy services using the latest techniques and technologies
  • Developing personalized treatment plans that consider each patient's unique needs and goals
  • Empowering patients with knowledge and self-management strategies for long-term health
  • Creating a supportive environment where patients feel heard, respected, and motivated
  • Continuously updating our skills and knowledge to provide the best possible care
