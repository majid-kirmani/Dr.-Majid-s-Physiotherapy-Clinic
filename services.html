Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy solutions for pain management and recovery

Advanced Physiotherapy Services

Professional care for optimal physical health and recovery

At Dr. Majid's Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of physiotherapy services designed to address various conditions and injuries. Our evidence-based approach combines traditional techniques with modern technology to provide the most effective treatments.

Each treatment plan is personalized to your specific needs after a thorough assessment. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and private treatment cabins to ensure your comfort and privacy during sessions.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques that involve skilled passive movements of joints and soft tissues to improve mobility, reduce pain, and optimize function. Our manual therapy approaches include:

  • Joint mobilization and manipulation
  • Soft tissue mobilization
  • Myofascial release
  • Trigger point therapy
  • Strain-counterstrain techniques

Electrotherapy

Advanced electrical modalities that use controlled electrical currents to stimulate tissues for pain relief, improved circulation, and accelerated healing. Our electrotherapy services include:

  • Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS)
  • Interferential Current Therapy (IFC)
  • Ultrasound therapy
  • Laser therapy
  • Electrical muscle stimulation

Therapeutic Exercise

Customized exercise programs designed to restore movement, build strength, improve flexibility, and enhance functional capacity. Our exercise programs include:

  • Resistance training
  • Stretching and flexibility exercises
  • Balance and proprioception training
  • Functional training
  • Posture correction exercises

Sports Rehabilitation

Specialized recovery programs for athletes and active individuals to facilitate rapid and safe return to sports and physical activities. Our sports rehab includes:

  • Sport-specific exercise programs
  • Functional movement assessment
  • Biomechanical analysis
  • Return-to-play protocols
  • Injury prevention strategies

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized treatments for conditions affecting the nervous system, focusing on improving movement, coordination, and function. Our neuro rehab services include:

  • Neurodevelopmental techniques
  • Gait training
  • Motor control retraining
  • Vestibular rehabilitation
  • Sensory integration therapy

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Tailored treatments for older adults to address age-related conditions, improve mobility, and enhance quality of life. Our geriatric services include:

  • Fall prevention programs
  • Osteoporosis management
  • Arthritis care
  • Balance training
  • Functional independence training
