Our Approach to Weight Management

Scientifically-backed methods for sustainable results

At Dr. Majid's Clinic, we understand that successful weight management requires a personalized approach. Our programs combine evidence-based nutrition, physical activity, and behavior modification strategies to help you achieve and maintain your weight goals.

With Dr. Majid's experience as an online consultant for HealthifyMe, we bring expert knowledge of nutrition science and weight management principles to develop sustainable plans that fit your lifestyle and preferences.

Our Weight Loss Programs

Tailored solutions for your weight management journey

Comprehensive Assessment

Our weight loss journey begins with a thorough assessment of your current health status, medical history, lifestyle habits, and weight goals. This helps us identify potential barriers and develop a personalized approach.

  • Body composition analysis
  • Metabolic rate estimation
  • Nutritional assessment
  • Physical activity evaluation
  • Goal setting and planning

Personalized Nutrition Plan

Based on your assessment, we create a customized nutrition plan that focuses on sustainable eating habits rather than restrictive dieting. Our plans emphasize whole foods and balanced nutrition while accommodating your preferences.

  • Calorie-controlled meal plans
  • Macronutrient optimization
  • Meal timing strategies
  • Grocery shopping guides
  • Recipe suggestions

Physical Activity Program

Exercise is a crucial component of successful weight management. We design activity programs that match your fitness level, preferences, and capabilities to ensure consistency and enjoyment.

  • Cardiovascular exercise prescription
  • Strength training routines
  • Flexibility exercises
  • Home-based workout options
  • Activity progression plans

Behavioral Support

Sustainable weight management requires addressing psychological aspects of eating and exercise. We provide strategies to help you develop healthier habits and overcome common challenges.

  • Mindful eating practices
  • Stress management techniques
  • Emotional eating strategies
  • Self-monitoring tools
  • Motivation enhancement

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our weight loss programs

How much weight can I expect to lose?

+

Healthy, sustainable weight loss typically ranges from 0.5-1kg per week. Our programs focus on gradual, steady progress rather than rapid weight loss, as this approach is more likely to result in long-term success and health benefits.

Will I need to follow a strict diet?

+

Our nutrition plans emphasize balance, moderation, and sustainability rather than severe restrictions. We work with your food preferences and lifestyle to create a plan you can maintain long-term, focusing on healthier choices rather than elimination of food groups.

How often will I need to exercise?

+

We typically recommend 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity activity per week, divided into manageable sessions. However, your specific exercise prescription will depend on your current fitness level, health status, and preferences.

How long does the program last?

+

Our standard programs range from 12-24 weeks, depending on your goals and starting point. However, we offer ongoing support and maintenance programs to help you sustain your results long-term.

Will I regain weight after completing the program?

+

Our programs are designed to help you develop sustainable habits that prevent weight regain. We provide maintenance strategies and follow-up support to help you navigate challenges and maintain your new healthier lifestyle long-term.
